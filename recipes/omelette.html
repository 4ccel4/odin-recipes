Easy Omelette

Easy omelette recipe that makes the perfect breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • Kosher salt and freshly gound black pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 a tablespoon olive oil, butter, or bacon grease
  • optional: add a couple tablespoons of your favorite veggies, meat, or cheese!

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk the eggs, salt, and pepper together until lightly frothy.
  2. In an 8-inch non-stick pan, heat the oil over medium-low heat. Pour in the eggs and tilt the pan to make sure they're evenly spread out, then turn the heat down to low. Cook the omelette for 2 to 3 minutes, or until the edges are cooked through and the top is slightly wet but not runny.
  3. If you'd like to add filling, add it to half of the omelette. Then use a patula to fold the empty half on top and slide the omelette onto a plate. Garnish with chopped herbs.

That's it! Enjoy!
